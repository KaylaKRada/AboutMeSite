Aspen & Ash

Let me introduce you to our beloved furry companions, Aspen and Ash. Aspen, a charming Australian Shepherd with a red merle coat, holds a special place in our hearts as our first-ever dog. Despite her royal demeanor, she's a true ball enthusiast and an avid fan of chicken nuggets and ice cream. Ash, on the other hand, is a mini Labrador with a personality as big as his love for snuggles. His affectionate nature is unmatched, and he'll do anything to be close to his family. With a healthy appetite and a willingness to lend a helping paw in the shop, he's a joy to be around.

Tucker Rada

Allow me to introduce my wonderful husband, Tucker - a man who embodies kindness, strength, and ambition. He's a true hands-on partner in life, and meeting him has completely shifted my perspective on relationships, marriage, and the world at large. I often tease him, saying that he should have been born in an era before electricity. We first crossed paths at a bowling alley, and he swept me off my feet with a proposal on the stunning Oregon coast. Our wedding took place at my grandparents' cabin - a place that holds a special spot in our hearts. Tucker is a wholehearted individual who approaches life with sincerity and passion. When you meet him, he'll warmly greet you with a handshake and ensure that your needs are met. I'm incredibly grateful to have him as my partner in life.
