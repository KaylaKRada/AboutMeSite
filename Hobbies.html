Twitch Streaming

I've always been a big fan of video games. It all started when I was in diapers - we had a Nintendo 64, a GameCube, and even a Wii. As I got older, my interest in gaming only grew. I started playing on PlayStations and Xboxes, and eventually, I built my own full computer. At first, I just played games for fun, but eventually, I started streaming on Twitch.com. It was a great experience, but it definitely came with a steep learning curve. If and when I get back into streaming go ahead and click the link and say Hi!

Gym & Lifting

I find myself thoroughly enjoying my daily trips to the gym, where I'm able to challenge myself physically and mentally while also finding a sense of inner peace. It's amazing how quickly my mood can improve after a good workout, and this positivity carries over into my day-to-day life. In fact, I've even started to embrace mornings thanks to the positive impact of exercise. I would highly recommend making exercise a regular part of your routine, as the benefits are truly remarkable.
